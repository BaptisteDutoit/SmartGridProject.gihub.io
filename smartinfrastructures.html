Smart Infrastructures

The transition of the rail system to an intelligent rail network meets a range of objectives covering the economy, society and the environment. Economically, it aims to control consumption and reduce energy bills.

Socially, it guarantees a steady supply of energy in a context of increasing rail traffic.

Environmentally, it aims to reduce carbon footprints.
Various strategies are being studied to make this vision a reality, including the development of hybrid trains with on-board energy storage, the integration of renewable energy production into stations and network infrastructure.

Smart Factories

Smart factories utilize advanced technologies such as the Internet of Things (IoT), artificial intelligence (AI), and robotics to optimize manufacturing processes.

These technologies enable real-time monitoring and analysis of production data, allowing for predictive maintenance, automated quality control, and efficient resource management.

By connecting machines, devices, and systems, smart factories streamline operations, reduce downtime, and increase productivity, ultimately driving innovation and competitiveness in the manufacturing industry.
Smart factories play a role in the smart grid by using advanced technology to manage energy efficiently.

They can adjust energy usage based on grid needs and participate in programs to balance energy supply and demand.

This helps make the grid more stable and supports the use of renewable energy sources.
